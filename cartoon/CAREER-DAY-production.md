# BIRD BURGER — "CAREER DAY"
### Episode 4 · ~30 seconds · 2026-07-21

Second smoke-break story (bookends Ep 3). Larry's first job: school lunch.
September 2001. He was in the room.

Universe rule applied (same as Epsine): every human becomes a bird. The
president = a **bald eagle** in a navy suit reading "MY PET WORM" to chick
students. The event stays entirely off-screen — the joke is the whisper chain
and the frozen page, never the towers. New cast: **the Aide** — grey pigeon,
black suit, earpiece, sunglasses.

---

## SCRIPT

**EXT. BACK ALLEY, NIGHT** — CAPTION: *"Smoke break. Hour 5."*
> EMPLOYEE: What was your first job?
> LARRY: School lunch.

**FLASHBACK — CLASSROOM, SEPIA** — CAPTION: *"September, 2001."*
Chick students on a reading rug. The eagle president on a tiny stool:
> PRESIDENT: The worm dug, and dug, and dug...
Larry stands at the side in a hairnet, holding a tray of burgers.

The pigeon aide enters. Whispers in the president's ear.
The reading stops mid-word. The president freezes, book still open.
The clock ticks. A chick coughs.

The aide crosses the room. Whispers in LARRY's ear.
> *(long pause)*
> LARRY: So... no lunch?
> PRESIDENT (tiny, shaken, still reading): ...and the worm went home.

**BACK TO PRESENT**
> EMPLOYEE: What did you do?
> LARRY: I kept serving lunch. *(beat, into camera)* Nobody ate.
The wall light pops and showers sparks. Neither reacts. Distant siren.

**SMASH CUT — LOGO CARD**
*"SERVING THROUGH HISTORY."*
