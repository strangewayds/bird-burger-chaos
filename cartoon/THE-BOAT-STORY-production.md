# BIRD BURGER — "THE BOAT STORY"
### Episode 3 · ~30 seconds · 2026-07-21

Larry ref: `larry-ref-v2.png` (mascot-accurate). New cast: **Epsine** — sleazy
older white seagull, navy captain's blazer, gold chain, sunglasses. He has a
boat. He will not stop talking about his island.

---

## SCRIPT

**EXT. BACK ALLEY, NIGHT**
Larry slumped on a milk crate by the dumpster. Cigarette. Beer.
CAPTION: *"Smoke break. Hour 3."*

> LARRY: I ever tell you about the boat?
> EMPLOYEE: What boat?
> *(Larry stares into the distance. Smoke drifts.)*

**FLASHBACK — YACHT DECK, SEPIA**
Larry in a flower lei with an umbrella drink, standing stiffly. Epsine
gestures at the horizon, talking non-stop.

> EPSINE: You gotta come to the island. Everybody who's anybody comes to the island.
> *(Larry sips his drink. Says nothing.)*
> EPSINE: Seriously. Why won't you come to the island?
> *(Very long pause.)*
> LARRY: I got work Monday.

**BACK TO PRESENT**
> EMPLOYEE: So... did you ever go?
> *(Larry takes a long drag, crushes the can, looks into camera.)*
> LARRY: No.

The dumpster bursts into flames. Neither bird reacts. Distant sirens.

**SMASH CUT — LOGO CARD**
*"WE DON'T TALK ABOUT THE ISLAND."*
