## SCRIPT

**EXT. DRIVE-THRU, OVERCAST DAY**
Duct-taped menu board, half the letters gone. One rusty car idles.
CAPTION: *"4 hours into a 30-minute shift."*

**AT THE SPEAKER**
> CUSTOMER: Hi, can I get a Number One?
> *(long static crackle)*
> LARRY (V.O., through speaker): We're out of that.
> CUSTOMER: ...Out of the Number One?
> LARRY (V.O.): Yes.

**INT. KITCHEN**
Larry scoops a grey patty from a dented bucket labeled **MEAT?** and slaps it
on the grill. It ignites immediately.
> EMPLOYEE: That's not the meat bucket.
> LARRY: It is now.

**EXT. DRIVE-THRU WINDOW**
Larry hands over a paper bag. The bag is smoking.
> CUSTOMER: Why is the bag smoking?
> LARRY: That's the flavor.
*(He retreats inside without breaking eye contact.)*

**FINALE**
> CUSTOMER: Can I get a refund?
Larry slaps a sticky note on the glass: **NO**. Slides the window shut.
The entire menu board collapses behind the car — sparks, dust, car alarm.
> LARRY (V.O., through speaker): Please pull forward.

**SMASH CUT — LOGO CARD**
*"OVER 4 CUSTOMERS SERVED."*
